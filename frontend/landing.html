job-application-assistant
AI-powered job applications

Tailored resumes and cover letters,
generated from your profile.

Paste any job posting. The app selects your most relevant experience, writes a targeted cover letter, and answers application questions — in seconds.

How it works
01 Build your profile once Enter your experience, projects, skills, and education. Write a few narrative responses the AI draws from when answering questions in your voice.
02 Paste a job posting Drop in text or a URL. The app extracts the role, required skills, responsibilities, and company context automatically.
03 Generate and download Get a tailored resume PDF, a cover letter that passes a five-dimension quality check, and answers to open-ended application questions.
What you get
Resume
Tailored to every role
Selects and orders your experience to match each job description. Compiled to a clean, ATS-friendly PDF via LaTeX. Fits a single page automatically.
Cover Letter
Generated and evaluated
Written against your actual candidate data — no fabricated metrics. Automatically scored on opening, depth, role fit, company specificity, and clarity. Retries until it passes.
Questions
Answered in your voice
Handles open-ended application questions using your profile and personal narrative context. Writes in first person, grounded in your actual experience.
